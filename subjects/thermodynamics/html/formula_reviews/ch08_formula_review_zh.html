Introduction to the Thermodynamics of Materials · David R. Gaskell · pp. 250–287

8.2–8.3 理想氣體與混合物

⭐ 壓縮因子 (8.1)
$$\boxed{Z = \frac{PV}{RT}} \tag{8.1}$$
Dalton 分壓定律 (8.2)
$$P_i = x_i P_{\text{total}} \tag{8.2}$$
⭐ 混合 Gibbs 自由能 (8.3)
$$\boxed{\Delta G_{\text{mix}} = nRT\sum_i x_i \ln x_i} \tag{8.3}$$
混合熵 (8.4)
$$\Delta S_{\text{mix}} = -nR\sum_i x_i \ln x_i \tag{8.4}$$
理想氣體 $\Delta H_{\text{mix}} = 0$ (8.5)
$$\Delta H_{\text{mix}} = 0 \tag{8.5}$$

8.4–8.5 真實氣體狀態方程式

⭐ van der Waals (8.6)
$$\boxed{\left(P + \frac{a}{V^2}\right)(V - b) = RT} \tag{8.6}$$
van der Waals 臨界點 (8.7)
$$T_c = \frac{8a}{27Rb},\ P_c = \frac{a}{27b^2},\ V_c = 3b \tag{8.7}$$
Redlich–Kwong (8.8)
$$P = \frac{RT}{V-b} - \frac{a}{T^{1/2}V(V+b)} \tag{8.8}$$
Virial 展開 (8.9)
$$\frac{PV}{RT} = 1 + \frac{B}{V} + \frac{C}{V^2} + \cdots \tag{8.9}$$

8.6–8.7 逸壓（Fugacity）

⭐ 逸壓定義 (8.10)
$$\boxed{d\mu = RT\,d\ln f \quad (\text{定溫})} \tag{8.10}$$
低壓極限 (8.11)
$$\lim_{P\to 0} \frac{f}{P} = 1 \tag{8.11}$$
⭐ 逸壓係數 (8.12)
$$\phi = \frac{f}{P} \tag{8.12}$$
從 $Z$ 計算 $\phi$ (8.13)
$$\ln\phi = \int_0^P \frac{Z-1}{P}\,dP \tag{8.13}$$
van der Waals 的 $\phi$ (8.14)
$$\ln\phi = \frac{b}{V-b} - \frac{2a}{RTV} - \ln\left[1 + \frac{b}{V-b} - \frac{a}{RTV}\right] \tag{8.14}$$
