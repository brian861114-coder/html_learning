第五章：基本方程式與其關係

Introduction to the Thermodynamics of Materials · David R. Gaskell · pp. 142–175

5.1–5.7 基本方程式與化學位

⭐ 含化學位的 $dU$ (5.1)
$$\boxed{dU = T\,dS - P\,dV + \sum_i \mu_i\,dn_i} \tag{5.1}$$
⭐ 含化學位的 $dH$ (5.2)
$$\boxed{dH = T\,dS + V\,dP + \sum_i \mu_i\,dn_i} \tag{5.2}$$
含化學位的 $dA$ (5.3)
$$dA = -S\,dT - P\,dV + \sum_i \mu_i\,dn_i \tag{5.3}$$
⭐ 含化學位的 $dG$ (5.4)
$$\boxed{dG = -S\,dT + V\,dP + \sum_i \mu_i\,dn_i} \tag{5.4}$$
⭐ 化學位的四個等價定義 (5.5)
$$\mu_i = \left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial n_i}\right)_{S,V} = \left(\frac{\partial H}{\partial n_i}\right)_{S,P} = \left(\frac{\partial A}{\partial n_i}\right)_{T,V} = \left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial n_i}\right)_{T,P} \tag{5.5}$$
係數關係 — 從 $dU$ (5.6a)
$$T = \left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial S}\right)_V, \quad -P = \left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial V}\right)_S \tag{5.6a}$$
係數關係 — 從 $dG$ (5.6b)
$$-S = \left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial T}\right)_P, \quad V = \left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial P}\right)_T \tag{5.6b}$$
$\mu_i$ 的 $G$ 定義 (5.7)
$$\mu_i = \left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial n_i}\right)_{T,P,n_{j\neq i}} \tag{5.7}$$

5.8–5.9 Maxwell 關係式

⭐ Maxwell 1 — 從 $dU$ (5.8)
$$\boxed{\left(\frac{\partial T}{\partial V}\right)_S = -\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial S}\right)_V} \tag{5.8}$$
⭐ Maxwell 2 — 從 $dH$ (5.9)
$$\boxed{\left(\frac{\partial T}{\partial P}\right)_S = \left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial S}\right)_P} \tag{5.9}$$
Maxwell 3 — 從 $dA$ (5.10a)
$$\left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial V}\right)_T = \left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V \tag{5.10a}$$
⭐ Maxwell 4 — 從 $dG$（最常用！）(5.11a)
$$\boxed{\left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial P}\right)_T = -\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial T}\right)_P} \tag{5.11a}$$
⚡ Maxwell 關係記憶法 — 熱力學方塊
$$ \begin{array}{c} S \quad U \quad V \\ H \quad A \quad G \\ P \quad T \quad - \end{array} $$ 對角變數的偏導數，箭頭方向決定正負號。從 $U$ 到 $G$ 經過 $A$ 帶來負號。

5.10 Maxwell 關係的應用

⭐ $C_P - C_V$ 一般關係 (5.12)
$$\boxed{C_P - C_V = \frac{TV\alpha^2}{\beta}} \tag{5.12}$$
$\alpha = (1/V)(\partial V/\partial T)_P$（熱膨脹係數），$\beta = -(1/V)(\partial V/\partial P)_T$（壓縮係數）。
內能的體積依賴 (5.13)
$$\left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial V}\right)_T = T\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V - P \tag{5.13}$$
理想氣體：$= 0$。真實氣體：$\neq 0$。
焓的壓力依賴 (5.14)
$$\left(\frac{\partial H}{\partial P}\right)_T = V - T\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial T}\right)_P \tag{5.14}$$

5.11–5.12 熵的全微分與 Gibbs–Helmholtz

⭐ 熵的全微分 (5.15)
$$\boxed{dS = \frac{C_P}{T}\,dT - \left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial T}\right)_P dP} \tag{5.15}$$
⭐ Gibbs–Helmholtz (5.16)
$$\boxed{\left(\frac{\partial (G/T)}{\partial T}\right)_P = -\frac{H}{T^2}} \tag{5.16}$$
⭐ Gibbs–Helmholtz — $\Delta$ 形式 (5.17)
$$\boxed{\left(\frac{\partial (\Delta G/T)}{\partial T}\right)_P = -\frac{\Delta H}{T^2}} \tag{5.17}$$
